What Makes Our Approach Different

We built TalariaTreadTracker because "500 miles and you're done" isn't how shoes actually wear out.

🏔️

Terrain-Aware Wear

A trail run wears shoes differently than asphalt. We auto-detect trail vs. road vs. treadmill via GPS elevation analysis and adjust wear accordingly.

Strava: counts 5 miles as 5 miles regardless of surface.

🧬

Biomechanics Impact

If your Apple Watch records cadence, ground contact time, or vertical oscillation, we use pace-aware targets to estimate how your form affects shoe stress.

Strava: no shoe-specific biomechanics analysis.

🔄

Rotation Bonus

Research shows rotating between shoes can reduce injury risk by 39%. We reward multi-shoe strategies with up to 35% wear reduction credit — because rested foam lasts longer.

Strava: tracks shoes independently with no rotation awareness.

🔬

Scientific Transparency

Every wear factor in the app links to peer-reviewed research with evidence quality ratings. We show our work, acknowledge limitations, and mark all estimates as estimates.

Strava: no methodology documentation for gear tracking.

🏃

Session Grouping

Warm-up walk, interval run, cool-down walk? We detect the full session automatically and assign the whole chain to one shoe — capturing your real workout pattern.

Strava: each activity is tracked independently.

🛡️

Privacy-First

All analysis runs on your device. No accounts, no external servers, no analytics. Your health data stays in your HealthKit and your personal iCloud — nowhere else.

Strava: account-based with data stored on Strava servers.
